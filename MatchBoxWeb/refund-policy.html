REFUND POLICY

Refund Eligibility

Subscription Refunds: Refunds for premium features are available within 48 hours of the original purchase.


Conditions for Refund: To be eligible for a refund, the user must demonstrate that they encountered a significant issue with the premium features that MatchBox Discord Bot failed to resolve.


How to Request a Refund

Contact Support: To initiate a refund request, contact MatchBox support via https://discord.gg/JsGFFQQJup.


Provide Information: When requesting a refund, please provide relevant details, including the issue encountered and any troubleshooting steps taken.


Refund Process

Refund Approval: Refund requests will be reviewed, and approval is subject to meeting the eligibility criteria.


Refund Method: Approved refunds will be processed through patreon.


Non-Refundable Circumstances

Change of Mind: Refunds will not be issued for purchases where the user has simply changed their mind.


Termination of Service: If MatchBox Discord Bot ceases operation, refunds will not be issued for unused premium features.

Contact Information

For refund inquiries or concerns, contact our support team at https://discord.gg/JsGFFQQJup.


Changes to Refund Policy

Policy Updates: This refund policy may be updated. Users will be notified of significant changes.
